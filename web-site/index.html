HTML5 logo in HTML5

An HTML5 logo generator made with <canvas> by taking the paths in the SVG logo and rewriting them as moveTo()/lineTo(). You can click on the image to open as PNG (thanks Anne).

If you don't see a slider below the logo, please try using another browser such as Opera for desktop.
